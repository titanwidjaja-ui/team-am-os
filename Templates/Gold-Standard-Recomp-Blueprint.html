Intensity Guidelines

Mechanical tension drives growth. Every set should be difficult. RPE 8 means you have 2 reps left. RPE 9 means 1 rep left. RPE 10 means true failure with good form. Given your low volume approach (1-3 sets per exercise), intensity must be high. If you're not approaching failure, you're wasting the set.

  • Compounds: add 5 lbs
  • Isolations: add 2.5-5 lbs or 1 rep

Every gram of carbohydrate holds 3-4g of water. On your 356g training days, that's almost 3 lbs of water weight. This isn't fat gain - it's functional hydration that makes muscles look full and perform better. Expect 2-3 lb swings between high and low carb days.

How Alcohol Affects Hydration

Not applicable to you, which is a massive advantage. You're avoiding one of the biggest recovery disruptors.

Symptom Adjustment Guide

  • Feeling puffy/bloated: Check sodium consistency - fluctuating intake causes more bloat than high consistent intake
  • Looking flat/stringy: Increase sodium by 500mg and reassess
  • Cramping: Usually low potassium relative to sodium - check food sources first
  • Headaches/fatigue: Classic low sodium symptoms - add 1/4 tsp salt to water and see if it resolves within an hour
