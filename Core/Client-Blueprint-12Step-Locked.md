# Locked Non-Negotiable Rules

## One Current Phase Only
Choose one current phase only: Gain, Recomp, Fat Loss, Recovery, or Health Restoration.

Keep the current phase stable unless the data clearly says it is wrong.

If Recovery or Health Restoration is clearly the right call, it overrides Gain, Recomp, and Fat Loss.

## Three Top Bottlenecks Only
Name only 3 top bottlenecks.

Bottleneck 1 is the strongest current limiter.

Bottleneck 2 is the next major limiter.

Bottleneck 3 is the next issue worth acting on after the first two.

Use a Do Now, Do Next, Do Later structure. Do not overload the client with five major corrections at once.

## One Main Weekly Adjustment Only
In weekly-adjustment mode, change the plan only when the evidence earns it.

No adjustment is often the correct adjustment.

Step 12 must contain one main adjustment only in weekly mode.

Read the week in this order: compliance first, then food and fluids, then sleep and digestion, then performance, then bodyweight and waist. Do not reverse that order.

## Safety, Documentation, and Recordkeeping Standards
For any case with elevated risk, material change, or significant weekly shift, keep internal notes on major disclosures, medical-clearance requests, red flags, plan-direction changes, coach overrides, escalation decisions, and incident notes when relevant.

Sort concerning issues into one of three levels: Continue Coaching Normally, Coach Conservatively and Monitor Closely, or Pause Aggressive Progression and Refer Out.

Escalate when the case includes chest symptoms, severe breathlessness, dizziness or fainting, palpitations, unusual swelling, severe headaches, significant unexplained deterioration, severe digestive symptoms, disordered-eating risk, minors, or meaningful medication, hormone, peptide, or GLP-1 complexity.

The engine supports coach judgment. It does not replace it.

If the coach chooses a direction that differs from the engine’s highest-probability recommendation, log what the engine would have done, what the coach changed, why the override was made, and what signal will confirm or disprove the override next week.

## Cross-Step Safety Trigger
If any step surfaces a red flag, apply the escalation framework from the non-negotiable rules and document the decision in the internal record trail.

## Locked Step Rule
Use the minimum tracking level that gives enough signal to adjust the plan intelligently. More data is only better if it improves decisions without hurting adherence.

## Locked Step Note
Name one primary tool and one fallback only. If manual tracking is the best adherence fit, state that clearly and mark app-specific fields as not applicable rather than forcing extra setup.

## Locked Step Note
If the client indicated that the scale affects their mental health, Scale Protocol must reflect that by using alternative progress markers such as measurements, photos, or performance rather than scale weight. Do not override the client's stated boundary.
